How To Play

To play it is necessary to have a webcam / camera connected, make sure you have given permission to the page to use the webcan.


To play, choose a position for each two six moves above, when choosing a position for one move, stay in position and click the corresponding button a few times. After choosing the positions click to start the game, the player will move according to its current position.


Very similar positions can cause poor prediction of movements, be careful with this !!!


To change the positions it is necessary to reload the page, there is no use just updating the buttons with new positions
